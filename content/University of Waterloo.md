Located in Ontario, Canada. 

I studied my **junior year** of Computer Science here. 

People around me are probably tired of hearing how much I love this university. I learned so much while studying here. I believe that the most important factor that makes a university great is its people. And both the professors and especially the students I met here are extraordinary.

## Extracurriculars
I was involved with the university **Computer Science Club**, **Culture and Language Exchange Club** (teaching Spanish and learning Korean from my friend Jai) and in the [[Ultimate Frisbee]] and Soccer intramural tournaments.

## Traveling
I was very fortunate to get to explore part of the beautiful nature and cities of Canada.

Some of the destinations include **The Thousand Islands** region, **Montreal**, **Toronto**, **Algonquin** Provincial Park (Ontario), **Niagara Falls**, **Calgary** and the stunning **Banff** National Park.
