I started playing Ultimate Frisbee during my time in Canada at the [[University of Waterloo]].
I played in the university intramural tournament for two terms, first with the _Flatearthers_ team and then with the _Individuals 3_ team.

In Spain, I have played with the team _Quijotes_ in Madrid. I won Spain's Ultimate Frisbee Championship (2nd division) with them.

During my time in Hong Kong, I was part of the [[HKUST]] Ultimate Frisbee team and Hong Kong's _Club 7_ team. I participated in several competitions with them, including the anual _BUCL_ tournament in Hong Kong (2nd place) and in Guangzhou (广州).
