The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

I studied my **senior year** of Computer Science and Engineering here.

## Extracurriculars
I was involved with the university **Rowing** team, **Ultimate Frisbee** team and **Softball** Team. I was also part of Hong Kong's ultimate frisbee team **Club 7**, with whom I competed in several tournaments across China.

- **Taiwan**: Taipei 台北 and Taichung 台中
- **South Korea**: Seoul 서울, Jeonju 전주, Busan 부산, Gyeongju 경주 and the beautiful Seoraksan 설악산
